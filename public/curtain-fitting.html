Our Process

How We Deliver Tailored Curtain Solutions

01

Consultation & Brief

We discuss fabrics, window styles, hardware preferences, and any automation requirements to plan the perfect installation.

02

Site Survey & Measuring

Detailed measurements capture fixing heights, returns, and clearance, ensuring your curtains and blinds fit exactly as intended.

03

Hardware Preparation

We source and prepare poles, tracks, brackets, and motorised components, matching finishes and weight ratings to your window dressings.

04

Professional Installation

Tracks are aligned, fixings secured, and fabrics dressed for perfect pleats and smooth operation, with optional motor configuration completed on-site.

05

Final Styling & Handover

We steam or dress fabrics as required, demonstrate operation, and share care tips so your curtains and blinds look exceptional from day one.

Mounting Expertise

Ready for Every Window & Surface

Brick & Masonry

We drill with diamond-tipped bits and use heavy-duty anchors to secure brackets into brick, stone, or concrete walls for long-term stability.

Plaster & Lathe

Fragile plaster walls are assessed and reinforced with specialist plugs and fixings that spread the load without cracking surfaces.

Drywall & Stud Partitions

We locate studs or use toggle anchors for cavity walls, guaranteeing that even weighty drapes and pelmets are securely supported.

Tiled Reveals

Precision drilling through tiles protects finishes while locking into the substrate beneath, ideal for bathrooms and kitchen windows.

Ceiling Mounts

We install ceiling-fixed tracks into concrete or timber joists, perfect for floor-to-ceiling curtains and room dividers.

Bay & Shaped Windows

Flexible tracks and custom-bent poles provide seamless coverage for sweeping bays and angled windows, with smooth gliding around every bend.
