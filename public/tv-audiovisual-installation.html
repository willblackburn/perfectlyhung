Our Process

How We Deliver Tailored AV Solutions

01

Consultation & Planning

We discuss your TV size, viewing preferences, AV equipment, and room layout to plan the perfect installation and positioning.

02

Site Survey & Measurements

Detailed measurements capture optimal viewing height, wall structure, and cable routing paths, ensuring everything fits perfectly.

03

Mount & Hardware Selection

We select the appropriate TV mount, brackets, and fixings based on your TV weight, wall type, and viewing requirements.

04

Professional Installation

Mounts are precisely aligned, securely fixed, and TVs are mounted with complete cable management. All AV equipment is connected and tested for optimal performance.

05

Final Setup & Handover

We configure settings, test all connections, demonstrate operation, and provide tips so you can enjoy your new AV setup immediately.

Mounting Expertise

Ready for Every Wall & Surface

Brick & Masonry

We drill with diamond-tipped bits and use heavy-duty anchors to secure TV mounts into brick, stone, or concrete walls for long-term stability and safety.

Plaster & Lathe

Fragile plaster walls are assessed and reinforced with specialist plugs and fixings that spread the load without cracking surfaces.

Drywall & Stud Partitions

We locate studs or use toggle anchors for cavity walls, guaranteeing that even large, heavy TVs are securely supported.

Tiled Walls

Precision drilling through tiles protects finishes while locking into the substrate beneath, ideal for bathrooms and kitchen installations.

Concrete & Block Walls

We install mounts into concrete or block walls using specialist anchors, perfect for heavy TVs and commercial installations.

Custom Configurations

We handle unique installations including corner mounts, above-fireplace positioning, and multi-TV setups with professional precision and care.
