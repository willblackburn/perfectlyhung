Expert Solutions

Handling All Wall Types with Professional Expertise

Every wall type presents unique challenges. Our team has extensive experience working with all surfaces, ensuring secure and damage-free installations.

Brick & Masonry

Solid brick and masonry walls require specialized drills and fixings. We use appropriate anchors and screws designed for maximum load capacity, ensuring heavy items like large mirrors stay securely mounted for years.

Plaster & Lathe

Older plaster walls need careful handling to prevent cracking. We assess wall condition and use appropriate fixings, including wall plugs designed for plaster to distribute weight evenly and prevent damage.

Drywall & Stud Walls

We locate studs using professional equipment and use toggle bolts or wall anchors for secure mounting when studs aren't available. Perfect for modern homes and apartments with hollow walls.

Tiled Surfaces

Specialized drill bits prevent tile cracking. We carefully drill through tiles and into the substrate, using appropriate anchors that won't damage your beautiful tiles while ensuring secure mounting.

Concrete & Block

Heavy-duty installations require concrete anchors or expansion bolts. We have the right tools and expertise to safely drill into concrete, ensuring rock-solid mounting for your heaviest items.

Specialty Surfaces

Glass, metal, and other unique surfaces require specialized techniques. We assess each situation and use appropriate methods to ensure secure installation without compromising surface integrity.
