Residential Installation

Expert Installation Services for Every Type of Residential Property

Perfectly Hung provides professional installation services for all types of residential properties across London. Whether you live in a modern new build, a grand stately home, a period property, or a charming countryside cottage, we have the expertise and care to handle your installation needs with precision and respect for your property's unique character.

Our experienced team understands the nuances of different property types—from contemporary apartments with stud walls to heritage homes with original plaster and lathe. We adapt our techniques and fixings to suit each property, ensuring secure installations that protect your home's structure and aesthetic integrity.

We work with homeowners, interior designers, and property managers to deliver picture hanging, TV mounting, curtain fitting, and mirror installation services tailored to your property type. Every installation is completed with meticulous attention to detail, using appropriate methods for your home's construction and period features.

Property Types We Serve

Expert Installation for Every Residential Home

New Builds & Modern Apartments

We install in contemporary new builds and modern apartments with stud walls, drywall, and cavity partitions. Our team uses appropriate fixings for modern construction methods, ensuring secure installations without compromising structural integrity.

Stately Homes & Grand Properties

We work with stately homes and grand residential properties, handling high ceilings, ornate features, and substantial wall structures. Our installations respect the architectural heritage while providing secure, professional results.

Period Properties & Listed Buildings

We specialize in period properties, Victorian, Edwardian, and Georgian homes, as well as listed buildings. We use specialist techniques and fixings that protect original features, plasterwork, and architectural details while ensuring secure installations.

Countryside Homes & Cottages

We install in countryside homes, cottages, and rural properties with traditional construction including stone walls, timber frames, and original features. Our approach adapts to each property's unique character and construction methods.

Townhouses & Terraced Properties

We work with London townhouses, terraced properties, and converted buildings. Our installations account for party walls, shared structures, and the specific challenges of urban residential properties.

Luxury Apartments & Penthouses

We provide premium installation services for luxury apartments and penthouses, handling high-value artwork, premium AV systems, and bespoke installations with the care and precision these properties demand.

Our Process

How We Deliver Tailored Residential Solutions

01

Property Assessment

We assess your property type, wall construction, and any period features or structural considerations that will influence our installation approach and fixings selection.

02

Consultation & Planning

We discuss your installation requirements, preferred positioning, and any specific considerations for your property type, ensuring the installation plan respects your home's character and structure.

03

Fixings & Method Selection

Based on your property type and wall construction, we select appropriate fixings, techniques, and installation methods that ensure secure results while protecting your property's structure and features.

04

Professional Installation

We complete the installation with precision, using laser levels for alignment, appropriate tools for each wall type, and careful techniques that protect your property throughout the process.

05

Final Check & Handover

We complete a final quality check, ensure everything is securely installed, and provide guidance on care and maintenance, leaving you with perfectly installed items and peace of mind.
