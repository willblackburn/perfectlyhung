About

LONDON'S TRUSTED PICTURE HANGING & ART INSTALLATION EXPERTS

Welcome to Perfectly Hung, your professional picture hanging and art installation service in London. With years of experience, we've helped hundreds of homeowners and businesses transform their spaces with expertly hung artwork, mirrors, and more.


Whether you're hanging a single family photo or installing an entire gallery wall, our fully insured team brings precision and care to every project. We specialize in picture hanging, mirror installation, TV mounting, curtain and blind fitting, and custom shelving for both residential and commercial properties across London.


Using professional-grade tools including laser levels and specialized fixings for every wall type, we ensure your items are perfectly aligned and securely mounted. We'll work with you to achieve the ideal placement and height, making your artwork and décor look their absolute best.


Need a professional picture hanger in London? Get in touch for a free quote or view our recent projects.

Pictures & Mirrors

We install pictures, mirrors and shelves with laser precision using the latest equipment to achieve the perfect outcome. With years of experience, we work with our customers to ensure the desired look is achieved.

 

Curtains & Blinds

We offer a full curtain/blind & shutter installation service. From single to multiple window installations, we can measure, order and fit curtains, blinds and shutters for any size window in any type of property.

 

TV & Audio Visual

We provide a comprehensive TV mounting service along with cable tidying solutions. We also provide bespoke TV furniture builds including wall mounted cabinets as well as floor standing units with LED lighting, if desired.

 

Residential

We provide our services to all types of residential houses and flats, large or small. From new builds to listed, historically protected buildings. All care is taken to prevent any structural damage that can occur with aging walls.

 

Commercial

Similarly we offer our services to commercial premises. From Cafe's, Restaurants and Public Houses to Museums, Cinemas and Art Houses. We can also provide our services for any Office Fit outs.
