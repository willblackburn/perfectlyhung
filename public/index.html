About

PICTURE HANGING DONE WITH LASER PRECISION

We provide a professional hanging service throughout London and the surrounding areas. We hang pictures, paintings, mirrors, curtains, blinds, shelves and TVs and we pride ourselves on the precision of our installations, using laser level and measuring equipment to ensure the end result is as close to perfect as possible. We can advise on the best placement for your items and we can carry out millimetre perfect installations to ensure your artwork is displayed with maximum visual effect.

Pictures / Mirrors

We install pictures, mirrors and shelves with laser precision using the latest equipment to achieve the perfect outcome. With years of experience, we work with our customers to ensure the desired look is achieved.

Curtains / Blinds

We offer a full curtain/blind/shutter installation service. From single to multiple window installations, we can measure, order and fit curtains, blinds and shutters for any size window in any property.

Audio Visual

We provide a comprehensive TV mounting service along with cable tidying solutions. We also provide bespoke TV furniture builds including wall mounted cabinets as well as floor standing units with LED lighting, if desired.
