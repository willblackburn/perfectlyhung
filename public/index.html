Welcome to Perfectly Hung, your professional picture hanging and art installation service in London. With years of experience, we've helped hundreds of homeowners and businesses transform their spaces with expertly hung artwork, mirrors, and more.

Whether you're hanging a single family photo or installing an entire gallery wall, our fully insured team brings precision and care to every project. Services

Picture & Mirror Hanging

Professional picture hanging and mirror installation service in London. We use laser-precision equipment to securely mount artwork, mirrors, and shelves on any wall type. Our experienced team ensures perfect placement and a flawless finish every time.

Curtains & Blinds

We provide professional curtain and blind installation services for homes and businesses across London. From measuring and ordering to expert fitting, we handle single windows or complete properties. Our service includes curtains, blinds, and shutters for any window size or property type.

TV & Audio Visual

Professional TV wall mounting with complete cable management solutions to keep your space clean and organized. We also create custom AV furniture including wall-mounted media cabinets and floor-standing units with integrated LED lighting for a premium home entertainment setup.

Residential

Our residential services cover all London homes and apartments, from modern new builds to listed heritage properties. We take special care with period features and aging walls, using appropriate fixings and techniques to protect your property's structure while ensuring secure, long-lasting installations.

Commercial

We work with commercial clients including cafes, restaurants, hotels, museums, galleries, and entertainment venues throughout London. Our commercial installation services also cover office fit-outs and corporate spaces, delivering professional results on schedule with minimal disruption to your business.
