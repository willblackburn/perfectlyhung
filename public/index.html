About

ART INSTALLATION SERVICES IN LONDON

We specialize in professional art installation and mounting services across London. Our expertise extends to hanging pictures, paintings, bespoke mirrors, curtains, blinds, shelves, and televisions. We are committed to precision, utilizing advanced tools like laser levels and measuring equipment to achieve flawless installations. Our team can provide expert advice on the optimal placement of your items, ensuring that your artwork and decor are displayed to maximize their visual impact.

Pictures & Mirrors

We install pictures, mirrors and shelves with laser precision using the latest equipment to achieve the perfect outcome. With years of experience, we work with our customers to ensure the desired look is achieved.

 

Curtains & Blinds

We offer a full curtain/blind & shutter installation service. From single to multiple window installations, we can measure, order and fit curtains, blinds and shutters for any size window in any type of property.

 

TV & Audio Visual

We provide a comprehensive TV mounting service along with cable tidying solutions. We also provide bespoke TV furniture builds including wall mounted cabinets as well as floor standing units with LED lighting, if desired.

 

Residential

We provide our services to all types of residential houses and flats, large or small. From new builds to listed, historically protected buildings. All care is taken to prevent any structural damage that can occur with aging walls.

 

Commercial

Similarly we offer our services to commercial premises. From Cafe's, Restaurants and Public Houses to Museums, Cinemas and Art Houses. We can also provide our services for any Office Fit outs.
