1. Scope of Work

Perfectly Hung ("the Company") provides picture hanging, art installation, mirror installation, and related services as agreed with the client prior to commencement of work. Any variations or additions must be confirmed by the Company before being undertaken.

2. Warranty

2.1 Coverage

The Company provides a warranty covering defects in the fixtures and fittings supplied and installed by us for a period of 12 months from the date of installation.

2.2 Warranty Limitations

The warranty applies only to:

  • Fixtures and fittings supplied and installed by the Company.
  • Issues arising from proven defects in materials or workmanship.

The warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by the client, third parties, or accidental impact.
  • Installations altered, adjusted, or removed by anyone not authorised by the Company.
  • Situations where the client insists on a specific placement or method contrary to the installer's professional recommendation.
  • Environmental changes (e.g., humidity or temperature shifts) affecting walls, fixings, or artwork.
