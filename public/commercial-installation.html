Our Process

How We Deliver Commercial Projects

01

Discovery & Briefing

We review drawings, schedules, and brand guidelines to scope the installation, define access requirements, and highlight specialist equipment or fixings.

02

Site Survey & RAMS

Detailed surveys capture wall types, services, and fixing limitations. We then issue risk assessments, method statements, and certificates for approval.

03

Scheduling & Coordination

We align with site managers, landlords, and other trades to agree access windows, delivery sequences, and permit requirements before work begins.

04

Professional Installation

Our installers deliver clean, precise work using laser alignment, dust extraction, and surface protection to keep spaces immaculate throughout.

05

Final Checks & Handover

We conduct a joint walkthrough, provide photographic evidence for remote teams, and leave you with maintenance guidance or follow-on support as needed.
